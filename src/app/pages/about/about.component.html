About me

Felipe is a Colombian based creative , with an eye for capturing not only the country of Colombia he calls home, but nearly every other beautiful location the world has to offer. 
His passions reside on Photography/Videography , music, literature, art, extreme sports and Artificial Intelligence applied to medicine, agriculture and environmental protection. 
His work is inspired by disciplines like philosophy, physics, design , mathematics and biology. 
He’s currently studying computer science at Universidad de los Andes, where he is developing his skills in algorithms , engineering , data structures, webpage and app development, IT businesses, programming languages and others.
